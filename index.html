Final-year University Project (2024)


My Final-year project was an Intra-vehicle Coffee Stabiliser and Anti-spill Device. The goal of the system was to prevent coffee spilling in a car when performing under normal driving conditions, such as braking, accelerating, and turning. It comprised of a sensor subsystem (Inertial Measurement Unit), a control subsystem (PID controller), as well as the physical actuation system (3-D printed platform and servo motors).

(PS, this project won me first place for final-year project in Electronic Engineering at the University of Pretoria :p)
